Our Stitching Process

Our simple four-step process ensures every piece of clothing is made with care, quality, and your personal needs in mind.

01

Consultation

First, we talk about the design you want. We help you choose the best cloth and a style that is perfect for your personality and the occasion.

02

Measurement

A meticulous set of measurements is taken to ensure an impeccable fit, forming the unique blueprint for your garment.

03

Creation

Our experienced tailors carefully cut the cloth and stitch your clothes by hand, using their years of experience.

04

Fitting & Finish

We do a final fitting and make any small changes needed to make sure it's comfortable and looks perfect on you.
